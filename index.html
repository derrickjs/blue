Blue

Blue is my favorite color for many reasons. The biggest reason would be the cool vibe it gives off. Blue is a naturally cool color. It looks good on cars and trucks alike. It is a soft relaxing color that reminds me of easy times like vacations.

For this assignment I used a color scheme generator. It gave me a list of 5 colors that center around a nice blue theme. The scheme uses 2 blues, a lighter “Blue Jean” blue, and a darker “Dark Cornflower Blue”. It also uses a shade of purple called “Midnight” and a lighter “honeydew” color. I feel like these colors complement each other well. Though I’m not the best at picking colors for color schemes the generators available help tremendously.

Colors can be used for all sorts of purposes online. They can show meaning such as a clicked link. They can be used to draw attention to things. A good red can give the viewer a warming feeling while a nice blue can give a cooling feeling. Colors can influence a user to take a certain action. A appealing color scheme can keep a user engaged.
